About Us

Welcome to The 1916 Company, where craftsmanship meets tradition. We are dedicated to providing high-quality products that embody the spirit of timeless elegance and sophistication.

Founded in 1916, our company has a rich history rooted in a passion for excellence. Over the years, we have become a symbol of craftsmanship, producing unique and distinguished pieces that stand the test of time.

At The 1916 Company, we believe in the art of creating not just products, but experiences. Our team of skilled artisans and designers pour their creativity and expertise into every detail, ensuring that each piece reflects our commitment to quality and refinement.

Explore our collection and discover the essence of The 1916 Company. Thank you for being a part of our journey.
